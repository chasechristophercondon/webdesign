How To Make an Iced Caramel Macchiato

In this article you will learn how to make an Iced Caramel
Macchiato in the style of how Starbucks makes them.


Iced Caramel Macchiato, (dreamstime.com)

Ingredients Needed

  • Vanilla Syrup
  • Ice
  • Milk
  • Espresso
  • Caramel Sauce

Directions

Starbucks serves its Ice Caramel Macchiato drinks in a layered fashion.
This is pretty fun and easy to do!

Start by putting a little vanilla syrup in the bottom of your cup,
then add ice so that the cup is about 1/2 full.

Slowly pour the milk over the ice, then add more ice so that your cup is almost full
(if I’m putting on a lid I will leave about 1″ of space so it doesn’t overflow).

Now slowly pour 2 shots of espresso over the ice. Take the caramel sauce and drizzle it all over the top.

And you're done! Good job!
